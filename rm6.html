R4M is the leading Engineering, Procurement, Construction, and Construction Management (EPCCM) company in the Philippines – spanning close to 5 decades of expertise and experience in building infrastructure, high-rise, commercial, residential, industrial, leisure, and mixed-use projects. MDC is an ISO-certified company, a Quadruple A Platinum contractor, and a wholly-owned subsidiary of Ayala Land, Inc., the largest property developer in the country.
